HOW TO PLAY?

Left and Right arrow keys to move, Space to shoot.

Press Enter to NUKE the screen!

Pick up PowerUps to upgrade your ship!

There are 3 difficulty settings:

Easy
Slow Moving Enemies
Slow Firing Enemies
Piercing Base Attack
+1 Life Per Round
3 Revives After Losing
Medium
Normal Moving Enemies
Normal Firing Enemies
No Piercing Base Attack
No Life per Round
3 Revives After Losing
Hard
Fast Moving Enemies
Fast Firing Enemies
No Piercing Base Attack
No Life per Round
Enemies Need 2 Hits to Destroy From Level 1
0 Revives after losing

Try to get a highscore and beat your friends!
